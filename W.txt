W: Hunting Posture

Coen can recast this ability in 3 seconds.

Preparing (first cast): Coen starts crawling and slows himself by 50%, this can last up to 3 seconds. If Coen is in bushes or Hiding, Coen becomes invisible.

Hunting (recast): Coen gains 30%/40%/50%/60%/70% move speed and 10%/12.5%/15%/17.5%/20% tenacity for 3-5 seconds (depends on the duration of Preparing).


Cooldown: 20/18/16/14/12 seconds
